Creamy Cavatappi

Ingredients

  • 8 onces (1/2 pound) cavatappi pasta
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more for the pasta water
  • 1/2 teaspoon finely ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup (1.3 ounces) finely grated parmesan cheese, plus more for garnish
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cup frozen peas, optional
  • 1/4 cup to 1/2 cup reserves pasta cooking water

Directions

  1. Boil pasta according to package.

  2. Sauce: Over medium heat add butter to a skillet. Once melted add garlic and cook until fragrant but not browned, about 30 seconds. Slowly add in the cream while strirring and season with salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer, reducing the heat. Stir unil slightly thickened, about 3 minutes.Add small amounts of parmesan stirring until melted. Stir in the lemon juice

  3. Drain the pasta and thaw the peas: Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water. Add frozen peas to a colander. Drain pasta over the the frozen peas (the hot pasta water will help thaw the peas.)

  4. Toss the pasta and serve: Add pasta and peas to the sauce. Add Reserved pasta water and turn the heat up to medium. Stir until you have a creamy sauce that coats the noodles. Sprinkle with some parmesan cheese and serve immediately.
